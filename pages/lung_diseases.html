Lung diseases




IPF:
Idiopathic Pulmonary Fibrosis is a chronic degenerative diseases of unknown etiology (10), derived from lung parenchyma changes caused by epithelial activation of fibroblast migration, proliferation and differentiation into active myofibroblasts that secrete large amount of extracellular matrix components (11).

IPF







COPD:
Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Diseases is a progressive, irreversible and debilitating disease with a heterogeneous phenotype and multi factorial causes in which tabacco smoke is consider the main one. COPD patients have respiratory insufficiency and identification of chronic bronchitis and emphysema (6).
